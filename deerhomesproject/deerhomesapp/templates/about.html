About us

Deerhomes® is a team of highly skilled professionals including Architects, Interior designers and Engineers sharing a deep passion for excellence, blending creativity with technical precision. We believe we can make life better for our clients though design. A well-designed space enhances the quality of everyday living. Be it a home or a commercial space, - circulation patterns, and creation of a comfortable micro environment is the result of careful planning, creative thinking, and flawless execution. By creating sustainable building modules using materials with a reduced carbon footprint, we try to create a better building system for the future generations. Top noch design and planning ensures that our projects create a better quality of life for our clients while efficiency in design is translated into cost savings in all the major projects of deerhomes. Our versatile design philosophy encompasses various architectural styles of the past to present so our porƞolio ranges from a contemporary minimalistic design approach to classical renaissance styles and traditional kerala architecture styles. Our project management team lead by experienced project managers, engineers and contractors ensure that the concepts and ideas originating from the drawing table is converted into built space flawlessly within the scheduled time. Deerhomes was founded in 1999 by Deepak Somarajan an architectural engineer from 97th batch of NIT Calicut, his wife Parvathy Vijayan a structural engineer from 2004 batch TKM Engineering college later joined the company as its director.
